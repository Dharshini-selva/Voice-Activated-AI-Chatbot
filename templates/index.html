Voice-Activated AI Chatbot

Speak or type your commands to interact with the AI assistant

Hello! I'm your AI assistant. How can I help you today?

Try these commands:

  • "Hello" or "Hi" - Greet the assistant
  • "What time is it?" - Get current time
  • "Search for python tutorials" - Search the web
  • "Wikipedia artificial intelligence" - Get Wikipedia summary
  • "Open YouTube" - Open YouTube in browser
  • "Goodbye" - End the conversation
